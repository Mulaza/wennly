At the moment, we do not offer free trials for the "Student" or "Pro" plans. However, you can explore the features available in the "Free" plan to get a sense of what to expect in the higher-tier plans.

Absolutely! You have the flexibility to cancel your subscription at any time. Once canceled, you will still have access to the plan's features until the end of the billing period.
